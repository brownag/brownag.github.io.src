This is a tutorial (worked examples and exercises) demonstrating use of the profileApply() function from the R package aqp.

http://ncss-tech.github.io/AQP/demos/profileApply/loafercreek.html

It loads a dataset, called loafercreek from the R package soilDB. The analysis is supported by the package sharpshootR. These packages are in large part developed by Dylan Beaudette, but there now are many contributors within NCSS.

Loafercreek the dataset is a collection of mostly moderately deep Ultic Haploxeralfs on metavolcanic rock from the CA630 soil survey area (parts of Calaveras and Tuolumne Counties.
